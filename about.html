About Donna

I'm a front-end developer based in Charleston, SC, with roots up and down the East Coast. Originally from Long Island, NY, I hold a B.S. in Event Management from George Mason University. After graduating, I built my own business as a social media manager and photographer, working with locally owned businesses here in Charleston.
After years of capturing visual stories and building brands, I felt ready for a fresh challenge. That led me to dive into the world of coding. Coding has been an exciting journey, blending creativity with problem-solving, and I’m thrilled to continue growing my skills in this field. Outside of work, you can often find me surfing—enjoying the waves and recharging along the coast.
